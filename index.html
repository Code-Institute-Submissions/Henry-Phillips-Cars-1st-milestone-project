About Us

Welcome to Henry Phillips Cars Ltd, a family run business that has been in the used car industry since 2006. James can help you with every aspect of your second-hand vehicle purchase. We work hard to make sure we understand our customers and their needs, ensuring Henry Phillips Cars offer a service which is honest, fair, and puts every customer first who visits us in person or online. We want people to come and enjoy the whole process, to see, touch, feel and even smell our used cars in stock, which is why James takes great pride in sourcing quality, individual second-hand cars.

If you are looking for great savings on quality used cars and mini’s in Suffolk area, then you have reached the right place. Henry Phillips Cars are a specialist used car and mini dealer based in Sudbury.

Meet the owner

James has always had a passion for cars this started when he was a small boy. Fascinated by racing and fast cars James’s time when growing up was filled with following formula One, British Touring Cars, Rallying and Single seater racing. James started to race professionally in 1997 at the age of just 27.

James has gained his experience in the industry working for local garages at the beginning of his career which saw him progress to large car manufacturer dealerships both in the workshops and the sales floor gaining over 30 year’s experience in the industry.
